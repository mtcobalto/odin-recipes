Chilean Completo

I do believe there is nothing more delicious in this world than a homemade completo, it is easy to do, you can have it for breakfast, lunch, or even dinner, and it is very cheap as well. So here I'll show you how to make one.

Ingredients:

  • 4 units of completo's bread
  • 4 sausages
  • 2 avocados
  • 1 tomato
  • 100g of sauerkraut

Steps:

  1. Cook the sausages in abundant water with a bay leaf, let boil for 5-7 minutes until the sausages look filled. Turn the fire off, and take the sausages out
  2. Cut the tomato in small cubes, without peeling it, and add a bit of salt. Grind the avocado and add salt. Heat the sauerkraut in a small pot, and meanwhile heat the bread a bit in the oven.
  3. When the bread is hpt, open on the side without cutting through the other side. Put the sausage inside, cover with sauerkraut, tomato on top, and finally avocado
  4. Put some mayonaisse or ketchup on top. Enjoy!
